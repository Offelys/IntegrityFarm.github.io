INTRO INTO GENETICS

Genetics

Is a fascinating field of study that immerses us into the world of heredity and variability of living organisms. It explores the mysteries of gene transmission from one generation to the next and reveals the influence of various factors on our genetic information.

Let's delve into some key concepts in genetics:

DNA(Deoxyribonucleic acid):

This is an incredible molecule that stores the genetic information of all living organisms. It forms a double helix structure, dancing together with the genes that determine our hereditary traits.

Genes:
